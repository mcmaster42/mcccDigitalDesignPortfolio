About Me

Hello! I'm Gary, a digital artist with a passion for observational humor and the absurd. Inspired by the legendary Gary Larson's "The Far Side," I create illustrations that find humor in the everyday and the extraordinary.

My work explores the humorous intersection between animals, humans, and the bizarre situations that arise when worlds collide. I'm particularly fascinated by how simple illustrations can tell complex stories and evoke genuine laughter.

When I'm not drawing, you might find me birdwatching, studying entomology, or researching obscure scientific facts that might inspire my next piece.

Skills

  • Single-panel Storytelling
  • Observational Humor
  • Adobe Creative Suite
  • Traditional Pen & Ink

My Artistic Philosophy

"I believe art should make you think, laugh, or preferably both at the same time. The best humor comes from seeing the world in new and unexpected ways."

I strive to create illustrations that capture those brief moments of absurdity that make life interesting. My goal is to create work that makes people pause, smile, and perhaps see their world a little differently.
